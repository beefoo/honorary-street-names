Yolanda Garcia Way (Bronx)
Present name:Third Avenue
Location:Both sides of the street, from 156th Street to 159th Street
Honoree: Yolanda Garcia was 53 when she died in February 2005. She was one of the founders of Nos Quedamos/We Stay, a community group that worked to prevent evictions of people who lived and worked in her Melrose neighborhood.
LL:2008/48
Yomo Toro Place (Bronx)
Present name:None
Location:At the intersection of Ogden Avenue and East 162nd Street
Honoree: Yomo Toro (1933-2012) was a virtuoso on the cuatro, a Puerto Rican stringed instrument, and an influential member of New York’s Latin music scene from the 1950s on.
LL:2013/50
Yvette Nicole Moreno Place (Bronx)
Present name:none
Location:Intersection of Castle Hill Road and Watson Avenue
Honoree: Yvette Nicole Moreno (b. 1976) worked at Carr Futures in the World Trade Center. She was killed in the terrorist attack of September 11, 2001.
LL:2003/34
